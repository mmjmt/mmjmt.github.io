Tubular Flow Reactor, TFR

Senior Laboratory Research

Abstract


This purpose of this experiment is to study the extent of dispersion effect on a tubular flow reactor by using axial dispersion model and to explore the degree of non-ideality of the tubular reactor in comparison to the ideal tubular reactor model (plug flow reactor) under such effect.

Additionally, the kinetics for a reaction of interest is also measured to further quantify the comparison between the two reactor models.
